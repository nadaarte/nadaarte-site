About Wenwen Cai

Wenwen Cai is a contemporary oil painter based in The Hague, Netherlands and Valencia, Spain. She focuses primarily on landscapes and portraits, using rich colors to express her affection for her surroundings and her intense emotions.

Her work explores the vibrant interplay of color and texture, inviting viewers to experience the depth of feeling behind each brushstroke.

Artistic Vision

Through her paintings, Wenwen captures the essence of identity and emotion, connecting the viewer with the materiality of the physical world.
